Join Social Boost and become a Booster!

Social Boost is a company formed by the YES Project to give NEET* 15-24 years olds the experience and skills required to run their own social media company.

Your mission as a Booster:

  • Learn how to use Social Media in a professional way
  • Learn about the different platforms and gain tips and tricks
  • Boost your entrepreneurial and sales skills
  • Learn how to plan a Social Media campaign
  • Take over the Social Media platforms for a real business
  • Help a local business boost their social media presence.
  • Look into starting up your own business in Social Media!
*Not in employment, education or training
